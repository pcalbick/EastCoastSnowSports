Camelback Mountain Resort

Today Camelback serves nearly 1,000,000 outdoor enthusiasts and visitors annually with 34 named trails, 16 lifts, 100% snowmaking, the largest snowtubing park in the U.S.A., Camelback Mountain Adventures (featuring the only mountain coaster in Pa, and North America’s longest twin 4,000 ft. Zip-Flyers) and Camelbeach Waterpark (the largest waterpark in Pa. Newly opened is the 453-suite Camelback Lodge and Aquatopia Indoor Waterpark featuring the largest indoor waterpark in the Northeast, adult and kids spas, and the ski-in/ski-out Trails End Pub and Grille. Imagine skiing and surfing in the same day! Combined with Camelback’s winter and summer operations, Camelback Resort is now the most interactive mountain resort in the U.S.A.

Location: In the Pocono Mountains at 301 Resort Drive, PO Box 168, Tannersville, Pa. 18372.
